The Best Movies According to Scoot

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Prestige

Psychological Thriller with a great cast. Set in a fun period, what more could you want?

Spirited Away

Immersive story telling blended with amazing visuals. Possibly the most influential anime movie of all time.

The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

The best of the LOTR trilogy, the epic journey begins in the shire and sets up the entire series.
